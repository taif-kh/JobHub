As a UI/UX Designer at PixelCraft Studios, you’ll be responsible for designing user interfaces that are not only visually appealing but also highly functional and user-friendly. You’ll work closely with our UX designers, developers, and product managers to bring ideas to life and create seamless digital experiences.  

Key Responsibilities: 
- Design visually stunning user interfaces for web and mobile applications. 
- Collaborate with UX designers to create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity mockups. 
- Develop and maintain design systems and style guides to ensure consistency across all platforms. 
- Work closely with developers to ensure designs are implemented accurately and efficiently. 
- Conduct user research and usability testing to gather feedback and iterate on designs. 
- Stay up-to-date with the latest design trends, tools, and technologies. 
- Present design concepts to stakeholders and incorporate feedback into final designs.  

Qualifications: 
- 3+ years of experience in UI design or a related field. 
- Strong portfolio showcasing your design skills and process. 
- Proficiency in design tools such as Figma, Sketch, Adobe XD, or Photoshop. 
- Solid understanding of user-centered design principles and best practices. 
- Experience creating responsive designs for web and mobile platforms. 
- Knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript is a plus. 
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills. 
- Ability to work in a fast-paced, deadline-driven environment.
